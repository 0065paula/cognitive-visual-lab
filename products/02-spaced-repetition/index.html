II. Observations · 观察 Observations
Obs. 1

遗忘先快后慢 Forgetting is Front-Loaded

50% 的内容在 1 小时内遗忘，但 1 天后遗忘速度大幅减缓。及时的第一轮复习最关键。 50% of content is forgotten within 1 hour, but the rate slows dramatically after 1 day. The first review is the most critical.

Obs. 2

间隔 > 连续 Spacing > Massing

同样的学习时间，分散在 10 天比集中在 1 天效果好 2-3 倍。连续阅读制造的是"熟悉感幻觉"。 The same study time spread over 10 days is 2-3× more effective than crammed into 1 day. Continuous reading creates an "illusion of fluency."

Obs. 3

提取 > 重读 Retrieval > Rereading

主动回忆（合上书本自测）比被动重读效率高 50%。测试不是检测工具，是学习工具。 Active recall (self-testing with the book closed) is 50% more effective than passive rereading. Testing is a learning tool, not just assessment.

Obs. 4

间隔要「刚好忘」 Review at the Edge of Forgetting

在"快忘但没全忘"时复习，记忆强化效果最佳。太频繁浪费精力，太稀疏前功尽弃。 Reviewing when you're "about to forget but haven't yet" yields the strongest memory consolidation. Too frequent wastes effort; too sparse loses progress.

Methods · 实践方法 Methods
1
当天睡前回顾——学习后 8-12 小时内做一次主动回忆，记忆留存率可从 40% 提升到 80%。 Review before sleep — Active recall 8-12 hours after learning boosts retention from 40% to 80%.
2
用闪卡工具自动化——Anki、RemNote 等工具内置间隔算法，自动安排最佳复习时间。 Use spaced repetition software — Anki, RemNote, etc. have built-in algorithms that schedule optimal review intervals automatically.
3
先自测，再翻书——强迫大脑在"困难模式"下提取信息，哪怕错了，学习效果也比直接看答案好。 Test yourself first, then check — Forcing your brain to retrieve in "hard mode," even if wrong, is more effective than immediately seeing the answer.
4
交错练习不同主题——不要一次只学一个章节，混着复习。大脑在切换中建立更牢固的连接。 Interleave different topics — Don't study one chapter at a time; mix reviews. The brain builds stronger connections through switching.
